Demon Slayer (Kimetsu No Yaiba 鬼滅の刃)

"Demon Slayer" is a Japanese anime that follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon. Alongside his friends, he embarks on a journey to find a cure for his sister and to avenge his family, all while facing powerful demons and uncovering dark secrets.

  • Inosuke Hashibira
    • Inosuke Hashibira is a wild, hot headed fighter recognized by the boar’s head mask he always wears. He’s aggressive and competitive, constantly rushing into fights and challenging others to prove his strength. Having grown up in the mountains, he developed a tough, instinct-driven personality and a raw, animal-like fighting style. Despite his reckless and over-the-top nature, he’s incredibly strong, adaptable, and deeply loyal to those he trusts. Fun fact this is one of Myelena's favorite character! :D
  • Tanjiro Kamado
    • Tanjiro Kamado is a compassionate and determined young swordsman who always puts his family and friends first. He’s just respectful and gentle by nature, but when it comes to protecting Nezuko or fighting demons, his willpower and strength shine through. His calm head and empathy often sets him apart, even in battle.

Solo Leveling(Na Honjaman Lebeleob 나 혼자만 레벨업)

"Solo Leveling" follows Sung Jinwoo, an ordinary young hunter in a world where humans fight monsters that emerge from mysterious gates. Despite starting out as one of the weakest, his life changes when he discovers a unique power that allows him to grow stronger without limits. The story follows his journey through dangerous dungeons, powerful enemies, and the secrets behind the gates, all while he steadily rises in strength and reputation.

  • Cha Hae-in
    • Cha Hae-in is one of the top hunters in South Korea and the only female S-Rank hunter in the story. She’s calm, disciplined, and respected for both her strength and her skill with a sword. Even though she has a quiet and serious personality, she stands out as someone who notices things others can’t, which plays an important role in how she connects with the world around her.
  • Sung Jinwoo
    • Sung Jinwoo begins as the weakest hunter, struggling to survive and often overlooked by others. After gaining a mysterious ability that lets him grow stronger without limits, he steadily transforms into a powerful figure capable of facing threats far beyond normal hunters. Though quiet at first, his determination and resilience drive his rise from the bottom to one of the strongest in his world.

The Seven Deadly Sins (Nanatsu no Taizai 七つの大罪)

"The Seven Deadly Sins" is a fantasy adventure about a once-feared group of knights who were accused of betraying their kingdom and then disappeared. Years later, when the kingdom faces new threats, a young princess sets out to find these warriors and bring them back together. The story follows their journey across the land as they uncover secrets, face powerful enemies, and fight to protect the kingdom.

  • Merlin
    • Merlin is the brains of the Seven Deadly Sins, known as the group’s powerful sorceress. Cool, confident, and mysterious, Merlin often keeps her true thoughts hidden, but when it comes to magic, she’s unmatched. Her knowledge and tricks make her just as dangerous as any fighter on the battlefield.
  • Hawk
    • Hawk is the talking pig who travels with the group, and he’s basically the comic relief but also surprisingly brave. Hawk is always cracking jokes or complaining about food, but when things get serious, he does his best to help his friends, even if he’s way smaller and weaker than the others.
